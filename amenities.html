    • SQUASH

      The game dates back to 19th century. Squash is a ball game played inside a four walled court. It can be played as singles or doubles. Even though squash is not an olympic sport,World Championships tournament is conducted and best player all over the world play for the grand tittle.

      In squash players take it in turns to hit the ball to the front wall where it must strike above the service line and below the out line and return to land in the opposite court back quarter to the one the player served or hit from, where their opponent will be waiting to return the ball. The ball isn't allowed to bounce before it hits the front wall but it is allowed to hit any of the other walls its way to the front wall.

      On the way back from the front wall the ball is allowed to hit any of the "in" areas of the court's walls but is only allowed to bounce ONCE on the court's floor, if it bounces more than once then the ball goes out of play and the player that failed to return the ball loses that rally.

      Rallies are played consecutively until a player wins the game. Three games must be won by a single player for that player to win the match.

    • VOLLEY BALL

      Volleyball is a team sport consisting of six players. Two teams play against each other separated by a net. The team with the leading game score wins the game. The game is started by one team serving the ball from the boundary line of the court to the other team over the net. The ball is passed from one team to other, the team which misses the ball loses the point and the other team gains one point. The complete rules are extensive, but simple. The main rule of the game is that the team may touch the ball up to 3 times but individual players may not touch the ball twice consecutively.

      Advantages of volleyball:

      ● Playing 45 minutes of volleyball can burn up to 585 calories

      ● improves muscle strength and tone

      ● strengthens the upper body, arms, shoulders, thighs, abdominals, and lower legs.

      ● volleyball improves hand-eye coordination, reflexes, and balance
